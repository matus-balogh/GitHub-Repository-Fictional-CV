MAX ROCKATANSKY

EXPERIENCE EQUIPMENT ABILITIES

Profile

Max Rockatansky is a Main Force Patrol (MFP) officer tasked with maintaining law and order on the roads of Victoria, Australia. Quiet, competent and with steadily increasing reputation. A family man.

Relevant Experience

Mad Max

Though the best officer on the force, he is secretly afraid that he is becoming as cold and heartless as the criminals he pursues.

  1. He reaches a breaking point when a gang of criminally insane bikers burn Max's partner alive
  2. Overwhelmed with grief and rage, he goes AWOL and systematically pursues and kills each gang member responsible
  3. After hunting down final gang member, Max drives out into the wasteland, leaving the fading remnants of civilization behind him.

Mad Max 2

In this film, set about three years after the first film, Max, while wandering the wasteland of the outback in his new home.

  1. He discovers a besieged group of people manning a remote oil drilling station and refinery
  2. Max agrees to help the refinery's denizens
  3. However, he still cannot form any significant bonds with others and chooses to continue wandering the wasteland alone
"I'm Just Here For The Gasoline." - Max Rockatansky

Mad Max Beyond Thunderdome

In this installment of the Mad Max series, it has been eighteen years since Max Rockatansky left his life behind him following his revenge killings.

  1. He has wandered through the outback, scavenging whatever he needed for survival
  2. He stumbles across a tribe of children living in a wilderness oasis, the descendants of survivors of a crashed airliner
  3. In this movie, Max is able to come full circle, regaining his humanity and soul once again

Mad Max: Fury Road

This movie is set sometime after Mad Max Beyond Thunderdome

  1. Max is captured by a gang known as the "War Boys", he is imprisoned and used as a live blood donor for the gang
  2. He escapes and helps a warrior, Furiosa, in her quest to help five women escape from the War Boys' despotic leader
  3. After the group defeat their pursuers, Max parts ways with the survivors of the group and disappears
